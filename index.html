Popular Karnataka Destinations

Coorg

Known as the "Scotland of India," Coorg offers rolling coffee plantations, misty hills, and gushing waterfalls. A perfect escape into nature with cool weather all year round.

Chikmagalur

A serene hill station surrounded by coffee estates and green valleys. Great for trekking, sunrise views, and a peaceful getaway from city life.

Udupi

A temple town famous for its spiritual heritage and world-renowned cuisine. Home to the historic Krishna Matha and nearby beaches worth exploring.

Gokarna

A peaceful coastal town with pristine beaches and ancient temples. Popular for its laid-back charm, away from the crowds of bigger tourist spots.

Murudeshwar

Home to one of the tallest Shiva statues in the world, set against the backdrop of the Arabian Sea. A must-visit for its temple and scenic coastline.

Mysore

The royal city of Karnataka, famous for the magnificent Mysore Palace, illuminated beautifully every evening. Explore royal heritage, gardens, and vibrant local markets.
